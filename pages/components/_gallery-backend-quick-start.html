1.Get familiar with the help features

  • Download the Quick Start PDF Guide from the top of this page
  • Review the Tutorials tab (especially Must Read Section)
  • Try some of the on-page blue "Tutorial" video buttons (great if you get stuck)
  • Understand how the asterisks (*) work - helps you focus on what’s important

2.Tell your story on your profile

  • Basic tab - enter your contact detail
  • Bio tab -upload a photo and tell us about yourself
  • Details tab - add your social media details, if you want
  • CV - you can add CV details
  • You can only add selected art after step 3 (below) so COME BACK TO THIS IN STEP 3

3.Create art records - the most important and time consuming step

  • Hit the green "Create Artwork" button in the artwork table
  • Start by adding title, and technique
  • Attach an image
  • Add details, size, description, age etc
  • Try using the clone button to speed up adding artwork
  • Go back to your profile and SELECT ART FOR YOUR PROFILE

4.Create and Publish Exhibitions

  • Hit the green "Create Exhibition" button on the Exhibition Table
  • Choose the type of exhibition you want (is it an event or a selection..?)
  • Choose a selection of artworks to attach to the exhibition (from step 3)
  • If you wish, add Exhibition Images, shots of an opening or your studio
  • Publish your exhibition - turn it on

5.Share your profile and exhibitions

  • On the first tab of your profile is your profile URL. Bookmark this on your phone. Now you have your personalized portfolio at your fingertips
  • You can use your profile URL as a basic website that has all your details beautifully formatted
  • Share specific Exhibitions via messaging or social media in three ways:
    • your mobile using the app
    • your desktop using the profile URL
    • the control panel using the share button on the exhibition table

6.Create your bespoke website

  • Hit the green “Create Website” button on the website builder tab
  • Choose a simple website name, extension and and type (probably Single Artist, you can change your mind later, so don’t worry)
  • Design and style it, choose a menu style, font and also add animations and a logo
  • Hit the green Create Page to button to add content
    • Single Exhibition pages allow clients/ followers to jump right into a selection of art
    • Multiple Exhibitions Page allow you present a selection of exhibitions
    • Bio/Profile/CV Pages adds to your story
  • Style pages by selecting the right mix of layouts and templates that deliver the perfect user experience

7.Connect your website to your domain

  • If you don’t have a domain yet, buy one (try GoDaddy or register.com)
  • Make sure you have a paid subscription with vPatina
  • Get in touch and we will get you connected…..
