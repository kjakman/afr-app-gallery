Artists Page Tutorial

This page allows you to showcase a number of artists, who could be students in a college, or artists in a collective.
To showcase an artist on this page they need to be in your network and give you permission to use their profile.
You can organise artists into two lists, primary and secondary (for example photographers and painters, or 1st year and 2nd year students)
1. To name the lists use the "Page Title" for the primary list and the "Title: List of Secondary Artists" for the secondary list
2. Add an artist to the list by typing their name (if you have permission to use their profile their name will autocomplete)
3. Choose a layout and template to style the page
