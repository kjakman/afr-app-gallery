Single Exhibition Page Tutorial

    1. Check "Show Most Recent Exhibition" and then choose which type of exhibitions to select for the page OR
    2. Check "Attach a Single Exhibition Manually" and then use the orange "Attach My Exhibitions" or "Attach Shared Exhibitions" button to select the exhibition you want for this page.
    3. Trouble Shooting : Don't forget to hit the Green "Save" button, so your changes stick. Make sure your exhibitions are published and have a Title Image, before trying to attach them. Make sure you also choose an Exhibition Template for the page.
